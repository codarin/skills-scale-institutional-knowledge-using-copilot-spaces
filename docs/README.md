# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to OctoAcme's project management process hub. This README is the central entry point for both newcomers and existing team members to quickly find practical guidance across the full delivery lifecycle.

OctoAcme follows a **customer-first, iterative approach**: we align early on customer outcomes, deliver in small increments, measure impact continuously, and use learnings to improve every cycle.

## Core principles

- **Clear ownership**: defined roles, decision-makers, and accountability throughout delivery.
- **Measurable outcomes**: goals, success metrics, and progress indicators are explicit and tracked.
- **Continuous improvement**: regular retrospectives and feedback loops improve both process and results.

## Process phases at a glance

1. **Initiation**: validate the problem, align stakeholders, and define measurable goals.
2. **Planning**: scope work, prioritize backlog, define milestones, and prepare delivery plans.
3. **Execution & Tracking**: deliver iteratively, monitor quality and metrics, and surface blockers early.
4. **Risk Management & Communication**: proactively manage risks, dependencies, and stakeholder communication.
5. **Release & Deployment**: prepare, deploy safely, verify outcomes, and communicate release status.
6. **Retrospective & Improvement**: capture learnings, identify actions, and improve future delivery.
